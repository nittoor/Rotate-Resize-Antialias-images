image1.rgb and image2.rgb are natural, colorful images. The corresponding .jpg files are also included for your reference.

image3.rgb is an image which has straight vertical black and white lines of 2 pixels each.

All these 3 images are of size 512x512 in the rgb format. It is a raw format that we use, in this case will be 512x512 r's followed by the same number of g and then b. Each r, g or b is one byte.
